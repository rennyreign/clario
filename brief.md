# ✅ Your Complete Website Brief: Clario

This is ready to hand to a designer/developer.

---

## **1. Project Overview**

**Brand:** Clario

**Tagline:** *The science of becoming yourself*

Clario is a premium identity-focused consulting offering designed for **high-functioning professionals who feel internally misaligned**.

The website is a **single-page experience** designed to:

→ clearly articulate the problem

→ position Clario as a structured solution

→ drive users to book a **paid consultation (Clario Fit – £50)**

---

## **2. Objectives**

- Primary: Convert visitors into **Clario Fit bookings**
- Secondary: Establish authority and trust
- Tertiary: Introduce structured pathway (Clario Core Eight)

---

## **3. Target Audience**

- High-performing professionals
- Stressed, overworked, externally functioning well
- Internally feel:
    - disconnected
    - misaligned
    - “not themselves”

They are:

- self-aware but stuck
- analytical / thoughtful
- willing to invest in clarity

---

## **4. Brand Direction & Tone**

**Tone:**

- Clear
- Calm
- Precise
- Grounded
- Slightly detached (in a good way)
- Not overly emotional or “healing” focused

**Avoid:**

- fluff
- clichés
- overly motivational language

---

## **5. Visual / Design Direction**

**Style:** Minimal & Clinical

- Neutral palette (off-white, soft greys, charcoal)
- Strong typography (clean, modern, editorial)
- High whitespace
- Structured layout

**Imagery (your photos):**

- Slightly desaturated
- Natural lighting
- Calm, composed expressions
- Mix of direct + slightly observational shots

---

**Goal:** Immediate recognition + action

### 2. Problem Section

**Goal:** Make the user feel seen

**Content (example tone):**

- You’re doing well on the outside
- But internally, something doesn’t align
- You’re tired, overextended, and disconnected from yourself
- You can’t explain it clearly — but you feel it

This section should feel:

→ direct

→ specific

→ slightly uncomfortable (in a good way)

### 3. Positioning Section (“Identity Architect”)

**Goal:** Differentiate you

**Content:**

- You are not a general coach
- You work with **identity, patterns, and structure**
- You help people understand:

    - where misalignment comes from
    - what needs to change

1. **Understand your patterns**
